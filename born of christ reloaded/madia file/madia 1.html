Meet Sophia

About Sophia

Sophia is a bright 8-year-old girl from a small village who dreams of becoming a doctor. Despite facing numerous challenges in her young life, she remains hopeful and dedicated to her studies.

Her Dreams

Sophia dreams of becoming a doctor to help others in her community. She wants to build a clinic in her village where everyone can receive medical care regardless of their financial situation.

Her Background

Sophia lives with her grandmother in a small house with no running water. Her parents passed away when she was young, and her grandmother struggles to make ends meet with her small farm.

Her Needs

Sophia needs support for her education, including school supplies, uniforms, and tuition. She also needs access to better nutrition and healthcare to help her thrive.
